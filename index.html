Buffalo Smudge

My name is William Brady and I'm a wildcrafting practitioner dedicated to harvesting, in properly observed manners, spirit-calling medicines for others. These are sometimes known as “vestiges," which may maintain and restore balance(s) in their lives and those they care about.

Wildcrafting is the art of perpetual learning, observing, and harvesting from nature, requiring year-round commitment. One of the ways commitment to nature works best is by receiving Creation's help from the ancient practices, along with dedication to spirit work.

I'm based out of Southern California, and it’s been an honor to be entrusted and guided in this work since 1997.
